Frequently Asked Questions

What is CallMate?
CallMate is an advanced calling and messaging platform offering reliable services with great features to manage your calls and contacts.
How can I top up my balance?
You can top up your balance using mobile payment methods like Bkash, Rocket, Nagad, and Upay. Simply visit the top-up page to add funds.
How can I change my account settings?
You can update your account settings by navigating to the Settings page where you can update your profile, notifications, and other preferences.
What should I do if I forgot my password?
If you've forgotten your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page to reset it via OTP (One-Time Password).
Can I contact customer support?
Yes, you can contact our support team through the Contact Us page. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.
